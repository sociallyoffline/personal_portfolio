EUROPE

Spain

La Sagrada Familia

When visiting Barcelona, Spain, do not miss the opportunity to see the Sagrada Familia. From its colorful stained glass windows to its towering columns,
arches, and intricate architectural details, this extraordinary basilica leaves visitors in awe at every turn.

2.Italy

Colosseum

Step back in time and explore the history of the world’s largest amphitheater, the Colosseum. With nearly 2,000 years of history, this impressive
structure offers a glimpse into the daily life and culture of the ancient Roman Empire.

1.France

Eiffel Tower

Experience the romance and charm of one of the most visited landmarks in the world, the Eiffel Tower. With its elegant design, breathtaking observation
decks, and dazzling light displays at night, it also serves as the centerpiece of major celebrations such as Bastille Day and New Year’s Eve in Paris.

ASIA

1.Japan

Mount Fuji

For adventure lovers and mountain climbers, Japan’s iconic Mount Fuji is a must-visit destination. As the country’s highest peak, it offers a challenging yet
rewarding experience, along with stunning panoramic views and a deep connection with nature.

2.China

Great Wall Of China

Visit one of China’s most famous landmarks, the Great Wall, a remarkable engineering achievement built in ancient times. As one of the greatest man-made
structures in history, it remains a powerful symbol of China and continues to amaze visitors with its spectacular views in every season.

3. Philippines

Boracay

Discover the stunning beaches of the Philippines, known for their long stretches of fine white sand, clear turquoise waters, and breathtaking sunsets. This destination
is perfect for those who want to unwind and enjoy a peaceful getaway.

SOUTH AMERICA

1.Brazil

Rio de Janeiro

A vibrant and energetic coastal city in Brazil, Rio de Janeiro is famous for its stunning beaches like Copacabana and Ipanema, iconic landmarks such as Christ
the Redeemer and Sugarloaf Mountain, and its lively culture expressed through music, dance, and world-famous festivals like Carnival.

2.Peru

Machu Picchu

A world-renowned ancient Incan citadel located high in the Andes Mountains, Machu Picchu is admired for its remarkably preserved stone structures, advanced
engineering, and deep historical and cultural significance, all surrounded by breathtaking mountain and cloud-forest scenery.

3. Colombia

Cartagena

A historic Caribbean city in Colombia known for its well-preserved walled old town, colorful colonial architecture, rich Spanish heritage, and vibrant coastal culture
blending history, food, and tropical charm.
